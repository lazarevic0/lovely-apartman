About Jovanovic's Apartman

Feel at home while on vacation. Enjoy this stunning view and the peaceful environment in Bijela. In this apartment couples and families up to 4 can enjoy their vacation close to Herceg Novi, Tivat and Kotor. The apartment is around 5 minutes walk from the closest beach in Bijela and provides a fully equipped kitchen and private parking.

The apartment is bright, clean and provides a queen bed and sofa bed with a madrace for comportable sleep. You'll also love to watch the sunsets on the balcony.

Where you’ll be

Herceg - Novi, Montenegro

Bijela is located on the Jadranska Magistrala which is the road connecting Igalo, Herceg Novi and Kotor. By car you can reach any of these destinations. The ferry is also close by connecting you to other cities like Budva.
The closest beach is about 5 minute walking and supermarkets, kiosks and restaurants are in walking distance too.

Getting around

If you’re coming by car then you have the most flexibility to going around the different places in Montenegro. Taxis are also very affordable in Bijela (around 1€ per km) and are recommended if you are not coming by car. There are public bus transports as well to Kotor, Tivat, Budva or Herceg Novi.
Coming with your bicycles is also possible as almost the entire road from Igalo to Kamenari is connected with a road next to the sea.
